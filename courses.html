Our courses

Welcome to Sperma's courses page, where you'll find a wealth of opportunities to deepen your understanding of God's Word and grow in your faith journey. Our courses are designed to be engaging, challenging, and practical, equipping you with the knowledge and skills you need to serve God and others.


At Sperma, we believe that learning is not just about acquiring information, but about transformation. Our courses are not just academic exercises, but life-changing experiences that can shape your character and destiny. Whether you're looking to explore the Bible for the first time, or to deepen your understanding of a specific topic, our courses will challenge you to think deeply, live authentically, and impact the world around you.


What sets our courses apart is our commitment to excellence in teaching, innovation in technology, and integration of faith and learning. Our faculty are not just experts in their fields, but passionate educators who are dedicated to helping you succeed. They will challenge you to think critically, engage creatively, and apply your learning to real-world situations.


Our courses are also designed to be flexible and accessible. We understand that life can be busy and unpredictable, so we offer courses in a variety of formats, including asynchronous online courses, synchronous virtual classrooms, and hybrid courses that combine online and in-person learning. You can choose the format that best fits your schedule and learning style.


So whether you're seeking personal enrichment, professional development, or ministry preparation, we have a course that's right for you. Browse our course offerings, connect with our faculty and staff, and take the next step in your journey of faith and learning. We believe that the seed of God's Word within you has the potential to grow into a mighty tree that bears fruit for the world. Let us help you cultivate that seed and bring forth a harvest of love, justice, and joy.
